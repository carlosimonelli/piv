Ventotene Integrated Project

In 1998 the Government has carried out the working program of the National Conference about Environment and Energy (ENEA); that devising plan activity has brought to the printing of the White Book, and of the related CIPE, which is a complex of guiding lines that are useful to bring the whole Italian system toward a global emissions level and a quality of life that are the ones that were stated in pot-Kyoto regulations.

The approach to that matters with an integrated methodology seems to be an excellent criterion to gain environment compatible objectives such as sustainable development:

* Integrated analysis of the needs of the territory;
* Integrated definition of the complex of useful procedures and technologies
* Integrated use, in time, of the whole complex of money founds, such as the ones that request nothing in change and the co-paid founds that are available from Regions, Town Councils, single Nations, European Community and private investors

Another very important point of this integrated approach is the involving of local, national and external actors that work on the single territories, who are able to successful implement what we said before.
This methodology, that goes toward the integration of the renewable energy sources technology, has been widely developed and sustained, by the National devising plans that brought to the printing of the White Book, by
ENERIN - Consorzio Ansaldo for Renewable Energies.

Starting from this point of view, in March 1999, the Town Ventotene Council, together with an expert people commission set up on purpose to work on Ventotene, has stated for its own area a devising procedure that comes from National procedures that are in force in Italy (Territorial Agreements, Area Contracts, P.R.U.S.S.T.), the aim of that statement is to gain environment-energy objectives in coherence with the commitment that the Italian Government has taken following the post-Kyoto guiding lines.

The Town Council, after the developing, through the P.I.V. Commission, of a pre-evaluation of the emergencies and of the problems existing in the area, has stated the launch of the Piano Integrato Ventotene (P.I.V.), this has to be used has a planning and attraction of public and private investor instrument, useful to bring the "Island System", in a four year time, to zero environment impact: that means zero Co2 emissions.

The Town Council has indicated as formal starting date April, 19 ,1999, when the formal task was given by the Council to the Commission.
The post-evaluations are to be made in a four year period of time.
